# Pack #2 Branding

Working name: **ECHO: Foundry Protocol**.

Other name ideas:
- ECHO: Factory Nexus
- ECHO: Gridworks
- ECHO: Foundry Line
- ECHO: Iron Relay

## Brand Position

Foundry Protocol is the operational factory experience for Pack #2. It should sell power routing, machine commissioning, logistics, and facility recovery.

## Visual Separation From Pack #1

- Pack #1: dust, survival, field warnings, orange hazard identity.
- Foundry Protocol: graphite factory panels, cyan instrumentation, amber caution tags, green throughput states.

## Palette

- Background graphite: `#15191b`
- Panel steel: `#232a2d`
- Instrument cyan: `#5bd6ff`
- Caution amber: `#f6c177`
- Throughput green: `#7ee787`
- Fault red: `#ff6b6b`

## Icon Style

Flat industrial emblem with a power node, conveyor arrow, and compact ECHO mark. Avoid skulls, dust storms, survival shelters, and horror imagery.
