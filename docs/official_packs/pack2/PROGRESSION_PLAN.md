# Pack #2 Progression Plan

This is a planning document around the 1.0.0 internal MVP. It does not authorize full Pack #2 gameplay beyond the first loop.

## First 30 Minutes

1. Open Terminal or Index and read the Pack #2 welcome guidance.
2. Complete Foundry Orientation with one Lens Deep Scan.
3. Claim the starter grid kit.
4. Place the hand crank generator, low voltage cable, and small battery bank.
5. Deep Scan the small battery bank.
6. Create a HoloMap waypoint for the processor worksite.
7. Claim and Deep Scan the Power Meter.
8. Deep Scan the hand crank generator.
9. Claim the Scrap Processor Blueprint and read the next objective.

## First 2 Hours

1. Stabilize generator, storage, and substation layout.
2. Commission the first processor route.
3. Add a depot and simple logistics request.
4. Unlock a multiblock workcell plan.
5. Add SoundCore warning cues and Index troubleshooting notes.

## Later Expansion

- Multi-floor facility routes.
- Convoy freight hooks.
- Larger factory contracts.
- Team/server production dashboards.
- Pack-specific resourcepack and screenshots.
