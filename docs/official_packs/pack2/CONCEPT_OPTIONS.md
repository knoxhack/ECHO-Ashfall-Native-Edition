# Official ECHO Pack #2 Concept Options

Decision: choose **Industrial Nexus Factory Pack**.

Public working name: **ECHO: Foundry Protocol**.

The chosen concept gives Pack #2 a clear identity that is not post-collapse survival: players rebuild a sealed industrial facility, stabilize power, commission machines, automate logistics, and expand production routes. It uses existing ECHO modules first and keeps all pack-specific claims in the Pack #2 profile/template.

## Chosen Concept

**Industrial Nexus Factory Pack** is selected as the active Pack #2 foundation.

Why:
- It is clearly different from Pack #1 without needing Pack #1 content.
- It reuses mature 1.5.x modules and the Tech template direction.
- It gives the launcher a strong second official experience: automation, factories, logistics, and operational clarity.
- It can start as metadata/profile/template planning without implementing new gameplay.

Pack #2 must remain internal until a real first 30 minutes loop, screenshots, and content validation exist.
