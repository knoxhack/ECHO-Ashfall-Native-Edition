# Pack #2 Art Requirements

This is an art brief only. Dedicated Pack #2 art is not required for the 1.0.0 MVP prototype.

Draft metadata may temporarily reference existing Industrial Nexus module art until dedicated assets exist.

## Required Before Public Launcher Visibility

- Square icon.
- Wide banner.
- Launcher card.
- Showcase image.
- At least five real gameplay screenshots from a live client.
- One module showcase image for the factory stack.

## Screenshot Ideas

1. Foundry Terminal dashboard.
2. Dormant substation Lens scan.
3. HoloMap factory floor layer.
4. Power spine with generator and battery bank.
5. First Industrial Nexus processor route.
6. Logistics depot request panel.
7. Multiblock workcell outline.
8. RuntimeGuard factory budget checklist.
9. Index machine troubleshooting page.
10. Server crew standing in the restored facility hub.

## Rejection Criteria

- No dust-storm survival hero art.
- No generic sci-fi corridor with no visible factory systems.
- No final public card until screenshots and MVP route exist.
