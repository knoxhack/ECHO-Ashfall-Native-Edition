# Official ECHO Pack #2 Identity

Status: 1.0.0 internal MVP lock.

## Final Working Title

ECHO: Foundry Protocol.

## Short Pitch

Restore a dormant industrial facility by commissioning power, machines, logistics routes, and operational dashboards with the existing ECHO module stack.

## Long Pitch

ECHO: Foundry Protocol is the second official ECHO pack profile. It focuses on controlled factory restoration rather than hostile survival escalation. Players begin inside a quiet facility with broken routing, incomplete power coverage, stalled processors, and unread maintenance notes. The opening loop asks the player to read system status, scan machinery, route power, create a waypoint, claim measured rewards, and see the next route while the Terminal, Index, HoloMap, Lens, TutorialCore, SoundCore, and WorldCore MVP content reinforces the same factory operations fantasy.

This profile has its own stakes, visual language, audio direction, mission structure, and pacing. Foundry Protocol is the factory commissioning prototype.

## Genre

Tech progression, factory restoration, logistics planning, light environmental narrative.

## Target Player

Players who enjoy power routing, machine chains, clean mission guidance, readable documentation, and building a production line that becomes more capable over time.

## Difference From Pack #1

- Pack #1 is survival-first; Foundry Protocol is commissioning-first.
- Pack #1 uses wasteland pressure and hazardous recovery; Foundry Protocol uses facility diagnostics, throughput, and restoration.
- Foundry Protocol has its own official profile, metadata, theme, launcher card, datapack namespace, and template.
- Foundry Protocol's mood is precise industrial recovery.

## Main Gameplay Loop

1. Check Terminal mission route.
2. Read the matching Index entry.
3. Scan the requested block with Lens.
4. Create or follow the HoloMap waypoint to the next commissioning point.
5. Complete a starter mission step.
6. Claim a small reward and unlock the next route.

## Visual Style

Dark factory panels, cool cyan instrumentation, warm caution amber, clean green throughput states, red fault states, hard-edged map overlays, compact icons, and restrained industrial surfaces.

## Audio Style

Low factory room tone, relay ticks, short confirmation stingers, warning pips for unsafe routing, and subtle UI sounds that feel like industrial instrumentation rather than horror or wilderness ambience.

## First 30 Minutes Experience

- Load the Foundry Protocol draft profile.
- Open the Terminal mission route or the Pack #2 welcome Index entry.
- Complete Foundry Orientation with one Lens Deep Scan.
- Claim and place the starter grid kit.
- Deep Scan the small battery bank.
- Create one HoloMap waypoint for the processor worksite.
- Claim the Power Meter, scan it, scan the hand crank generator, and receive the next objective.

## First 2 Hours Experience

- Review the first processor-line handoff.
- Review depot request planning.
- Review runtime budget guidance before expanding machine rows.
- Validate that all pack-specific assumptions remain in the Pack #2 profile/template.
- Capture missing art, audio, and mechanic requirements for a later MVP.

## Endgame Direction

The future endgame should move toward facility-scale production orchestration, multi-line throughput balancing, controlled expansions, and high-level logistics routing. No endgame implementation is included in 1.0.0.
